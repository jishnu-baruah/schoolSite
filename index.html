About

ST. XAVIER'S SCHOOL was established in 1992 and it is private and unaided. It is located in the BARBARUAH block of the DIBRUGARH district of Assam. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 10. The school is Co-educational and it has an attached pre-primary section. English is the medium of instruction in this school. This school is approachable by an all-weather road. Thes academic session starts in January.
The school has a Private building which has more than 25 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It also has some other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Headmaster/Teacher. The school has a Pucca boundary wall. The school is fully electrified and a minimum of two fans in each room. Purified drinking water is available for students. The school has separate washrooms for boys and girls. The school has 2 playgrounds. The school also has a library. The school has over 25 computers for teaching and learning purposes.
